## OpenAI + Malta: ChatGPT Plus for every citizen <a id="openai-malta"></a>

**Source:** [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/malta-chatgpt-plus-partnership/) · [Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/next/2026/05/16/malta-offers-free-chatgpt-plus-access-to-its-citizens-through-a-national-ai-program) · **Type:** launch · **Time (UTC):** 16 May 2026

OpenAI and the Government of Malta announced a partnership under the **OpenAI for Countries** initiative that gives every eligible Maltese resident one year of ChatGPT Plus access at no personal cost. To qualify, residents registered with Malta's national digital identity system must complete *AI for All*, a free online course developed by the University of Malta. Distribution is managed by the Malta Digital Innovation Authority; Maltese citizens living abroad are also eligible. Phase one launches in May 2026; financial terms were not disclosed.

The arrangement is the first national-government deal to tie ChatGPT Plus access to a structured AI literacy requirement rather than a flat subsidy.

**Why it matters:** This is the first instance of a sovereign government deploying ChatGPT Plus as a state-provided utility — setting a template for the OpenAI for Countries programme. The literacy gate (course completion) is a notable design choice that distinguishes it from previous government AI pilots and may influence how other nations structure similar agreements.
