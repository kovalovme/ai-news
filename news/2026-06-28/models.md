# Models — 2026-06-28

## ByteDance Seed 2.1 Pro and Turbo <a id="bytedance-seed-21"></a>

**Source:** [ByteDance Seed Blog](https://seed.bytedance.com/en/blog/seed2-1-officially-released-advancing-ai-productivity) · **Type:** release · **Time (UTC):** announced Jun 23, released Jun 24

ByteDance's Volcano Engine FORCE Conference (June 23) unveiled Seed 2.1 Pro and Turbo — the next generation of the DouBao model series — with GA on June 24. The models are purpose-built for agentic workloads across three capability planes: coding engineering delivery (1539 on Code Arena Frontend, #8 globally, near Claude Opus 4.6), general agent task execution (top on GDPVal, competitive on Workspace Bench and Agent Startup Bench), and multimodal understanding (strong on TVBench, Video MME, and TOMATO). Seed 2.1 Pro targets enterprise full-cycle development — requirement analysis, implementation, bug fixing, environment setup, and result validation. Both Pro and Turbo are available through DouBao and the Volcano Engine API.

**Why it matters:** ByteDance's aggressive push on agent-task benchmarks offers a credible non-US option for teams affected by the Fable 5 / Mythos 5 access disruption; the coding and workspace-agent performance puts it squarely in competition with Claude Opus 4.6 for enterprise coding workflows.
