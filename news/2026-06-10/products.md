## Claude Fable 5: Multi-Platform Rollout and June 22 Subscription Deadline <a id="fable-5-platform-rollout"></a>

**Source:** [GitHub Copilot changelog](https://github.blog/changelog/2026-06-09-claude-fable-5-is-generally-available-for-github-copilot/) · [AWS Bedrock](https://aws.amazon.com/blogs/aws/anthropic-claude-fable-5-on-aws-mythos-class-capabilities-with-built-in-safeguards-now-available/) · [Azure Foundry](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/claude-fable-5-is-now-available-in-microsoft-foundry-powering-the-next-era-of-autonomous-agents/) · **Type:** launch · **Time (UTC):** 2026-06-09

Claude Fable 5 went GA simultaneously across five channels on June 9: the Claude API (model ID `claude-fable-5-20260609`), Amazon Web Services Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, Microsoft Azure Foundry, and GitHub Copilot (available to Copilot Pro+, Max, Business, and Enterprise plans). Augment Code also announced same-day availability for its IDE coding extension.

A hard subscription transition takes effect on June 23, 2026. From June 9 through June 22, Fable 5 is included in Pro, Max, Team, and seat-based Enterprise Claude plans at no additional charge. Beginning June 23, Fable 5 on those plans converts to usage-credit billing at full API rates ($10/$50 per million input/output tokens) with no rollover. GitHub Copilot plan pricing follows its own schedule. A new 30-day data retention requirement covers all Mythos-class traffic for Anthropic safety analysis; retained data is not used for model training.

**Why it matters:** The simultaneous five-platform launch is the broadest same-day managed-cloud rollout Anthropic has executed. The June 22 cutoff is a concrete planning deadline: teams evaluating whether to keep Fable 5 as default or fall back to Opus 4.8 for cost control have 13 days from today to decide before billing changes.
