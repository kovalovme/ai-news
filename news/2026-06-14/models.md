# Models — 2026-06-14

## GLM-5.2 <a id="glm-52"></a>

**Source:** [Zhipu AI / Z.ai](https://z.ai) · [AI Weekly](https://aiweekly.co/node/2946) · [AIToolly](https://aitoolly.com/ai-news/article/2026-06-14-zhipu-ai-releases-glm-52-a-fully-open-source-frontier-model-featuring-a-1m-context-window) · **Type:** release · **Time (UTC):** Jun 13, ~12:00

Zhipu AI released GLM-5.2 on June 13, 2026 for users on its GLM Coding Plan (Lite/Pro/Max/Team). The model is built on the same 744B-parameter Mixture-of-Experts backbone as GLM-5 but extends the context window from 200K to a genuine 1 million tokens, supports up to 131K output tokens per response, and introduces a dual thinking-effort system (High and Max) designed for long-horizon agentic tasks. No benchmark figures were published at launch; independent evaluations are pending. The standalone API, Z.ai chatbot access, and MIT-licensed open weights are scheduled for the following week. The announcement drew 551 points on Hacker News within 24 hours.

**Why it matters:** An MIT-licensed 1M-context model at frontier scale lands the week after US export controls knocked Fable 5 and Mythos 5 offline for non-US users. GLM-5.2 directly fills that gap for engineers outside the United States who need a capable, unencumbered open-weight model for coding and agent workloads — no access restrictions apply.
