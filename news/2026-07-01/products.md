# Products — 2026-07-01

## Claude Science <a id="claude-science"></a>

**Source:** [Anthropic via TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/30/anthropics-claude-science-bets-on-workflow-not-a-new-model-to-win-over-scientists/) · **Type:** launch · **Time (UTC):** Jun 30 ~00:01

Anthropic launched Claude Science, a specialized research workbench built on top of existing Claude models (including Opus 4.8) rather than a new model. The platform integrates more than 60 scientific databases and ships prebuilt toolkits for genomics, protein structure prediction, and computational chemistry. A separate fact-checking agent verifies citations and calculations before results are exported. Claude Science runs within the existing Claude interface, accessible in beta to Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise subscribers.

Anthropic is also offering a grant program: up to 50 teams can receive $30,000 in usage credits. Applications close July 15, with award notifications on July 31.

**Why it matters:** Rather than competing on raw model capability for scientific tasks, Anthropic is competing on workflow integration — removing the need for researchers to manually chain databases, notebooks, and pipelines. The fact-checker layer directly targets the hallucinated-citation problem that has slowed adoption in regulated domains like drug discovery and clinical research.
