# Products — 2026-06-04

## Anthropic launches Services Track and Partner Hub for the Claude Partner Network <a id="claude-partner-network-services-track"></a>

**Source:** [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/services-track-partner-hub) · **Type:** launch · **Time (UTC):** June 3, ~13:00

Anthropic formalized the services side of the Claude Partner Network — first announced in March 2026 alongside a $100M investment in partner training and marketing — with a tiered Services Track and a public Partner Hub directory. The three tiers measure actual production work rather than just partnership agreements:

| Tier | Certified practitioners | Production deployments | Public stories |
|------|------------------------:|-----------------------:|---------------:|
| **Select** | 10+ | 2+ | 1+ |
| **Preferred** | 100+ | 15+ | 3+ |
| **Global Premier** | 1,000+ (in 3+ regions) | 100+ | 15+ |

Since March, 40,000+ firms have applied and 10,000+ consultants have earned a Claude certification. Firms already scaling: Accenture (30,000 practitioners on the Claude track), Deloitte (470,000), Cognizant (350,000). Promotions run twice a year (January 1, July 1), with a bonus review on October 1 for the first year. The Partner Hub is a live directory where customers can filter by tier, region, and deployment type.

**Why it matters:** The tiering is unusual in that all firms — regardless of size — face identical requirements based on shipping and deploying, not just signing paperwork. Engineers at partner firms get a structured training and certification path, and the dashboard gives daily visibility into advancement criteria, which is useful for planning Claude-related practice growth.
