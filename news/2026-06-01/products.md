## Runway establishes London European HQ with $200M+ UK pledge <a id="runway-london-hq"></a>

**Source:** [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/01/nvidia-backed-runway-london-expansion.html) · [The Next Web](https://thenextweb.com/news/runway-london-european-headquarters) · **Type:** launch · **Time (UTC):** —

Runway announced on June 1 that London will serve as its European headquarters, accompanied by a commitment to invest more than $200 million into the UK AI ecosystem through the end of 2028. Co-CEO Anastasis Germanidis cited proximity to existing enterprise customers — BBC, Fremantle, and WPP — and an already-active London research team as the rationale. The company did not break down how the $200M will be spent or provide a hiring target.

Runway most recently raised $315M in a Series E led by General Atlantic, with participation from AMD Ventures and Nvidia, at a $5.3 billion valuation. The announcement follows similar UK commitments from Anthropic (London office for 800 employees, announced April 2026) and OpenAI (first permanent UK office). The UK government has been actively courting US AI labs to anchor London as a European AI hub.

**Why it matters:** Runway's London expansion, combined with the $5.3B valuation and founding membership in NVIDIA's Cosmos Coalition announced the same week, positions the company as a key node in the emerging physical-AI and video-generation ecosystem. The $200M pledge is largely symbolic at this stage — no timeline or breakdown — but the co-location with BBC and WPP signals a clear enterprise-media go-to-market focus for European operations.
