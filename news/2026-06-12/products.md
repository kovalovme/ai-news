## Anthropic Claude Corps <a id="anthropic-claude-corps"></a>

**Source:** [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-corps) · **Type:** launch · **Time (UTC):** 2026-06-11

Anthropic launched Claude Corps, a national fellowship program that places early-career professionals inside US nonprofits as full-time AI practitioners. Fellows receive an $85,000 annual salary plus benefits, 12 months of structured Claude training (including weekly 5-hour instruction from CodePath), direct technical access to Anthropic engineers, and a large token budget for agentic work. Eligibility requires only US work authorization, basic Claude familiarity, and fewer than two years of full-time work experience — no degree requirement. Host organizations span insurance, social services, education, and public health nonprofits in Florida, Texas, North Carolina, Illinois, Arkansas, Indiana, and Ohio, among others. The first cohort of 100 fellows begins October 2026; applications close July 17. Subsequent cohorts follow in January and August 2027, with a stated target of 1,000 fellows across 400+ nonprofits. The program is funded through Anthropic's $150 million commitment announced alongside the "Policy on the AI Exponential" paper on June 10.

The program is structured as a three-way partnership: Anthropic funds and designs training, CodePath serves as the legal employer and runs curriculum, and Social Finance measures outcomes and manages scale.

**Why it matters:** Claude Corps is Anthropic's public acknowledgment that AI will displace early-career knowledge workers and an attempt to redirect that disruption toward nonprofit mission delivery. For the AI ecosystem, it creates a pipeline of practitioners trained specifically on agentic Claude workflows — extending adoption into sectors (nonprofits, government, healthcare) that lack the resources to build AI teams independently.

## DXC Technology–Anthropic Multi-Year Global Alliance <a id="dxc-anthropic-alliance"></a>

**Source:** [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/news/dxc-anthropic-alliance) · [DXC newsroom](https://dxc.com/newsroom/06112026-dxc-and-anthropic-announce-multi-year-global-alliance-to-bring-ai-into-mission-critical-enterprise-systems) · **Type:** partnership · **Time (UTC):** 2026-06-11

DXC Technology and Anthropic announced a multi-year global alliance to bring Claude into the mission-critical systems DXC operates for the world's largest banks, airlines, insurers, manufacturers, and government agencies. DXC will selectively recruit and certify tens of thousands of engineers through a 90-day Anthropic Partner Academy curriculum, granting them persistent daily access to Claude and progressively deeper proficiency tiers in designing, deploying, and governing agentic systems. The program targets initial production deployments in three sectors: insurance, cybersecurity, and application services.

The alliance builds on existing DXC use: DXC's **OASIS** orchestration platform — its AI-native managed-services layer, launched April 2026 and deployed across 50+ customers — was built primarily with Claude, with the company reporting an estimated 10× acceleration in software delivery and more than 95% of code passing through Claude before human review.

**Why it matters:** DXC's installed base is the legacy IT substrate for the global regulated enterprise — the stack that actually runs airline ticketing, banking ledgers, and insurance claims. This partnership moves Claude from greenfield AI-native products into the brown-field core, where most of the world's enterprise data actually lives. For Claude's competitive position, replacing incumbents at this layer is more durable than winning new AI-first accounts.
