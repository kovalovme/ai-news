## Anthropic opens Seoul office; signs MOU with Korea MSIT and six enterprise deployments <a id="anthropic-seoul"></a>

**Source:** [Anthropic newsroom](https://www.anthropic.com/news/seoul-office-partnerships-korean-ai-ecosystem) · [Korea Times](https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/amp/business/tech-science/20260618/anthropic-opens-seoul-office-to-expand-ties-with-korean-ai-ecosystem) · [UPI](https://www.upi.com/Top_News/World-News/2026/06/18/korea-Anthropic-Seoul-office-Korea-partnerships-Washington-AI-export-controls/4641781769900/) · **Type:** launch · **Time (UTC):** Jun 17–18

Anthropic formally opened its Seoul office on June 17, its third Asia-Pacific location after Tokyo and Bengaluru. KiYoung Choi leads it as Representative Director, Anthropic Korea. Simultaneously, the company announced an MOU with South Korea's Ministry of Science and ICT covering AI safety evaluation in the Korean language and cybersecurity cooperation. Six enterprise deployments were announced alongside the opening:

| Customer | Deployment |
|---|---|
| NAVER | Claude Code across entire engineering organization |
| Samsung SDS | Claude Cowork + Claude Code across Samsung Electronics |
| LG CNS | Claude across LG Group |
| Nexon | Claude Code for live-service game development |
| Hanwha Solutions | Claude via AWS Bedrock (in-region data controls) |
| Channel Corp | Claude powering Channel Talk (230,000+ businesses) |

Additionally, Anthropic will provide Claude access to up to 60 researchers from the National AI Research Lab consortium (KAIST, Korea University, Yonsei, POSTECH).

**Why it matters:** The expansion represents Anthropic's largest single-market commercial push in months, announced in the same week Washington's export control ban took Fable 5 and Mythos 5 offline globally. The timing underscores an apparent geographic hedge — deepening APAC enterprise ties while the US government limits the company's frontier model availability outside US borders.
