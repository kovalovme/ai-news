## Sesame iOS App — Public Preview <a id="sesame-ios-preview"></a>

**Source:** [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/28/sesame-the-conversational-ai-startup-from-oculus-founders-launches-its-ios-app/) · [Sesame announcement](https://www.testingcatalog.com/sesame-debutes-ios-app-in-preview-with-personal-voice-agents/) · **Type:** launch · **Time (UTC):** May 28

Sesame — the conversational AI startup co-founded by former Oculus executives — opened public preview of its iOS app in 39 countries. The app ships with four distinct voice agents (Maya, Miles, Simone, Charlie), each with individual voice, personality, and persistent memory. Sesame runs parallel web searches mid-conversation and integrates results into responses in real time. During beta, users added search cards with image results, notes capture, text mode, and "deep dive" extended-answer sessions. The previous research preview of Maya and Miles accumulated over one million users. The full experience is currently free with a short sign-up waitlist. The company's longer-term target is AI-enabled smart eyewear, planned for 2027.

**Why it matters:** Sesame is the most technically detailed public demonstration of real-time voice AI with parallel retrieval since GPT-4o Voice. The four distinct character model distinguishes it from single-persona voice assistants; the 39-country launch at free pricing sets up a rapid user acquisition push ahead of a likely monetization shift.

## Mistral AI Now Summit: Vibe for Work, Voxtral, Robostral <a id="mistral-now-summit"></a>

**Source:** [Summit notes by Koen van Gilst](https://koenvangilst.nl/lab/mistral-ai-now-summit) · **Type:** launch · **Time (UTC):** —

At the Mistral AI Now summit, the company announced **Vibe for Work** — a direct competitor to Claude for Work targeting enterprise knowledge-work subscriptions. Also highlighted: **Voxtral**, a multilingual voice model now powering Amazon Alexa+ in Europe; **Robostral**, an industrial robotics model built with ASML targeting computational tasks in semiconductor manufacturing; and **Document AI**, an OCR system deployed by the EU Patent Office for large-scale patent processing. A live demo showed Codestral decoding ancient Egyptian papyri from scanned images, with researchers estimating the work would have taken 2,000+ years manually. Mistral also emphasized its full-stack positioning: owned compute (40MW Paris data center), on-premises deployment, and enterprise partners including BNP Paribas and Abanca.

**Why it matters:** Vibe for Work entering the same market segment as Claude for Work and Google Workspace AI signals that European AI is moving beyond "open-source alternative" framing toward direct enterprise SaaS competition. The Voxtral / Alexa+ deal is the most significant voice AI distribution partnership announced by a non-US lab this year.
